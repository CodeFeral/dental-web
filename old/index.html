Georgi Bagdatov

Devoted to dental excellence.

  • Professional Dental Expertise

    Rooted in a legacy of dental excellence, Dr. Bagdatov upholds the tradition of quality dental care pioneered by his father. His practice skillfully combines time-honored methods with contemporary dental techniques.

  • Educational Background

    Dr. Bagdatov earned his dental degree from the Medical University of Sofia, where he was thoroughly educated in dental sciences and modern clinical practices.

  • Professional Approach with a Personal Touch

    With his extensive experience, Dr. Bagdatov focuses on delivering comprehensive dental services, ranging from preventive care to complex procedures. He is known for his patient-first approach, ensuring comfort on each visit.

  • Professional Dental Care

    Dr. Bagdatov's practice is adept at delivering a wide spectrum of dental services with precision and care. He is committed to providing high-quality, tailored dental treatments that meet the individual needs of each patient.
